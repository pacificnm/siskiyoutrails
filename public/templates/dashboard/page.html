Welcome To Siskiyou Trails!

Located in southwestern Oregon and extending into California, the Forest ranges from the crest of the Cascades Mountains west into the Siskiyou Mountains, nearly to the Pacific Ocean. The Forest covers almost 1.8 million acres; portions of the Applegate and Illinois River drainage's extend into northern California. The Rogue River drains over 75 percent of the Forest's land area. The forest contains approximately 400 miles of trails for hiking, biking, and horseback riding.

All trail data on Siskiyou Trails is member contributed. Our site is free to use and free to join all we ask is that you contribute information about the trails listed on this site.
